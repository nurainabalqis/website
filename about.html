Hi, I'm Nur Aina Balqis

I'm a Computer Science student at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM) with a strong interest in front-end web development and UI/UX design. I love crafting clean, interactive, and accessible websites that blend functionality with aesthetic appeal.

My journey into the tech world started with curiosity and creativity, which quickly grew into a commitment to mastering web technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and design tools. I enjoy solving real-world problems through elegant and user-friendly interfaces.

Beyond code, I'm deeply inspired by collaboration and continuous learning. Whether it’s building projects in class, contributing to group work, or exploring new frameworks during my free time, I’m always eager to grow and push my boundaries.

Currently based in Negeri Sembilan and studying in Skudai, I balance academics with tech events, and creative pursuits. When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me experimenting with new design trends, journaling ideas, or enjoying a cup of kopi while sketching UI mockups.

Looking ahead, I hope to work in roles that combine technology and human-centered design, helping to build inclusive digital products that make life easier and more enjoyable.
